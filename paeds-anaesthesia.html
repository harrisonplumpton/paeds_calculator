▸ Reference sources & formulas used

Weight estimation (APLS 2016):

  • Neonate (0 months): 3.5 kg
  • 1–11 months: 0.5 × age(months) + 4
  • 1–5 years: 2 × age(years) + 8 (= 2 × (age + 4))
  • 6–12 years: 3 × age(years) + 7

Height estimation: Piecewise formula validated against WHO 50th centile data. 0 months: 50 cm; 1–12 months: 50 + months × 2.08 cm; 1–2 years: 75 + (months − 12) cm; 2–12 years: 87 + (years − 2) × 6.5 cm.

ETT size: Uncuffed: age/4 + 4 (rounded to nearest 0.5 mm). Cuffed: age/4 + 3.5. Oral depth: ID × 3 (<1 year) or age/2 + 12 cm. Nasal depth: oral + 3 cm.

Drug doses: Drawn from BNFc, APLS 2016, and standard paediatric anaesthetic references. Where ranges are shown, dose is calculated at the midpoint for display — clinical judgement should be applied. Concentrations shown reflect standard preparations; local availability may vary.

CVC insertion depths: Right IJV depth = height(cm) ÷ 10 − 1 cm (Andropoulos formula). Left IJV = height ÷ 10 + 1 cm. Femoral = weight(kg) + 5 cm (rough guide; verify with fluoroscopy or CXR).

Maintenance fluids (Holliday–Segar): First 10 kg at 4 mL/kg/hr; next 10 kg at 2 mL/kg/hr; each kg above 20 at 1 mL/kg/hr.

Minimum systolic BP: Neonate >50 mmHg; children >1 year: 70 + (2 × age) mmHg. These are minimum thresholds, not targets.

Built as a quick reference aid. Not a regulated medical device. Use clinical judgement.
